Overview

Purpose

The purpose of White Water Rafting is to provide our clients with a thrilling yet fun and safe way to enjoy traveling down the river. To help achieve this, we'll provide all the necessary equipment and experienced guides needed to navigate and experience the white waters of local rivers. To bring awareness to our company, this site will be designed to help build our business by attracting new clients. The site will outline what clients can expect on rafting trips. We will include photos of our guides and share their years of experience and a personal statement. We will also include client photos along with their testimonials.

Audience

Our site is targeting individuals as well as large and small groups of family, friends, or company teams. We are able to accommodate clients from ages 5 to 95. Clients must be in good health and can have any level of experience. We will not only target the thrill seekers who are adventurous, but also those who are seeking a relaxing scenic trip down the river. When clients book a trip with us, they can be sure we will work with them to fulfill their expectations. Clients most likely will access this site from their desktop, laptop or any mobile device.
